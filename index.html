Project Overview



An human resource management app is a technology that helps you automate and simplify your complex HR processes and manage them without any human intervention.



Idea



It is one of most commonly used web-based & Mobile application that enables individuals to securely and Conveniently connect to HR department online via a laptop, tablet, smartphone, computer and other web-connected device. instead of going to meet office for communication to HR.

User Centered Design Process


In each phase of the design process, designer focus on users and their needs which is known as user-centered design (UCD). UCD’s design team uses a number of research and design methods to involves users throughout the design process, creating in a highly usable and accessible product for them.
